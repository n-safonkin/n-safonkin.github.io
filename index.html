About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Mathematics at Leipzig University. I completed my PhD at the University of Reims Champagne-Ardenne in 2023. I am interested in noncommutative algebra, representation theory, and algebraic combinatorics, with connections to noncommutative Poisson geometry and deformation quantization.
