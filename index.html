About

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Mathematics at Leipzig University. I completed my PhD at the University of Reims Champagne-Ardenne in 2023.

I am interested in noncommutative algebra, representation theory, and algebraic combinatorics, with connections to noncommutative Poisson geometry and deformation quantization.

My CV is available here: CV. My papers can also be found on my arXiv author page.
